Welcome to the Wilderness

A restored cottage nestled in shaded sand dunes alongside Lake Michigan. A place to slow down, open up, and settle in.

The Cottage

A family project, carefully restored and quietly modern.

Originally built in 1976 and renewed in 2026, Trail’s End honors its architecture and natural surroundings. Beneath a canopy of hardwoods, steps from the Lake Michigan shoreline, it’s a home filled with history and heart.

The Architecture

Three stories in the trees.

Dark vertical siding and crisp white windows set the cottage against the green of the ravine. A wooden boardwalk carries you from the drive to the front door, through ferns and dune grass.

Inside, warm wood runs floor to ceiling — open staircases, elevated decks, and flowing transitions between levels that reward a slower pace.

The Setting

Sunsets over the big lake.

Forest & Lakeshore

Wild, quiet, and close to the water.

The cottage sits in a lush, natural environment shared with local wildlife — deer, turkeys, woodpeckers, and the occasional fox. Wooded trails wind from the yard toward the dunes and the public shoreline beyond.

Mornings are for coffee and birdsong; evenings for the long, golden light that pours through the trees off the lake.

Thoughtfully Equipped

Modern comfort, lightly worn in.

Fresh air & efficiency

A whole-house energy recovery ventilator keeps fresh air moving while capturing energy savings, and a whole-house water filter serves every tap.

Peace of mind

A Generac propane generator stands ready for inclement weather, and a UniFi camera system covers the property perimeter for added security.

Connected, on your terms

Starlink internet is controlled here at the cottage, so the signal can be switched on or off — stay reachable, or unplug completely.

Sleeps six

New beds and mattresses, updated bathrooms, and modern appliances throughout — the essentials refreshed, the character preserved.

Sound & cinema

A Sonos system plays through the living area and kitchen, with a projector tucked in the living-room cabinet for movie nights.

Coffee, properly

A French press, drip maker, and burr grinder are stocked and ready for a great cup on a slow morning.
